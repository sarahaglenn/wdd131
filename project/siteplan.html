Site Name: Explore Skellefteå

The name represents that this site is a place for collected information about Skellefteå, Sweden.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information to tourists about things they can do while visiting Skellefteå.

Scenarios

  • What outdoor activities are available?
  • What cultural things can I do?
  • What are some good dining options?
  • When is the best time of year to visit?
  • Where can I stay when I come to visit?
